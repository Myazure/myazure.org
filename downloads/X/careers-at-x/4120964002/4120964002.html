Operations Manager, X

Program Management

Mountain View, CA (HQ)

Team:

This role is for a confidential, early-stage moonshot technology.

Responsibilities:

  • Plan, execute, and manage in-field data collection campaigns for experiments designed by the Science, Engineering, and Management teams.
  • Establish test sites at various locations throughout the US. This may include designing and setting up in-field support systems (e.g. data connectivity, charging stations, storage spaces, etc), and communicate testing resources available back to the engineering teams. Work with EHS to ensure the health and safety requirements are met for field operations.
  • Ensure data collected meets the requirements of the software team. Ensure high uptime of data collection platforms. Support in field troubleshooting of data collection platforms and help conduct reliability studies.
  • Hire, train, and manage TVCs who will be performing data collection in remote locations. Ensure performance of quality work. Update or develop SOPs to train others to use them.
  • Depending on time of year, travel expected 50-75% of the time.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Completion of an engineering degree (BSc) or equivalent experience
  • 2+ years of experience developing electro-mechanical and/or robotic systems, and working with engineers, designers, and builders to develop new systems. Experience working in startup and/or R&D environments.
  • 3+ years in an operations role.
  • 5+ years of experience in customer and stakeholder management, as well as managing external vendors
  • Must be able to lift 50 lbs, work outdoors, stand or walk for extended periods of time.
  • Willing to travel for data collection locally (California Bay Area, Central Valley and Central Coast), across the US, and potentially internationally

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MSc. in Engineering (Robotics degree preferred) + 2 years experience
  • Experience with projects that involve Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, or Machine Learning
  • Demonstrated ability to self-manage, take initiative, problem-solve, and able to navigate through ambiguity.
  • Experience working in agriculture is a plus
  • Interest or experience working with photography is a plus
  • Conversational fluency in Spanish strongly preferred
