X, The Moonshot Factory

X is a diverse group of inventors and entrepreneurs who build and launch technologies that aim to improve the lives of millions, even billions, of people. Our goal: 10x impact on the world’s most intractable problems, not just 10% improvement. Our projects have the riskiness and aspiration of research, and we approach them with the focus, speed, and ambition of a startup.
